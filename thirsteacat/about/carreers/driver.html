If you are a responsible driver with a passion for supporting a great cause and enjoy delivering exceptional service, we encourage you to apply for the Delivery Driver position at THIRSTEA CAT. Join us in delivering wellness, one cup at a time.

Key Responsibilities:
Safely transport tea products to designated locations.
Ensure the accurate and timely delivery of orders.
Inspect and maintain the delivery vehicle to ensure it operates efficiently.
Provide exceptional customer service during deliveries.
Maintain delivery records and logs.
Assist in loading and unloading tea products.
Adhere to all traffic and safety regulations.

Qualifications:
Valid driver's license.
Proven experience as a delivery driver is a plus.
Excellent driving record.
Strong time management and organizational skills.
Ability to work in a team.
Physical stamina and strength for lifting and carrying.
Customer service-oriented with good communication skills.
Knowledge of local routes and streets.

Benefits:
Competitive salary.
Opportunity to be part of a socially responsible company.
Employee discounts on THIRSTEA CAT products.
Positive and supportive work environment.
