If you are a passionate cook with a flair for infusing flavors and enjoy creating unique tea-inspired dishes, we encourage you to apply for the Tea-Infused Cook position at THIRSTEA CAT. Join us in crafting extraordinary tea-infused meals that bring joy to our customers.

Key Responsibilities:
Develop and execute recipes for a variety of tea-infused dishes, both sweet and savory.
Prepare and cook tea-infused meals, ensuring unique and delightful flavors.
Experiment with tea blends to create innovative culinary experiences.
Maintain high standards of food quality and presentation.
Collaborate with the team to create new tea-infused recipes.
Keep inventory of kitchen supplies and ingredients.
Adhere to safety and sanitation standards in the kitchen.

Qualifications:
Proven experience as a cook, chef, or relevant culinary role.
Creative flair for developing unique and flavorful recipes.
Knowledge of tea blending and infusion is a plus.
Strong organizational and time management skills.
Attention to detail and precision in cooking.
Ability to work in a team and maintain a clean workspace.

Benefits:
Competitive salary.
Opportunity to be part of a socially responsible company.
Employee discounts on THIRSTEA CAT products.
Positive and supportive work environment.
