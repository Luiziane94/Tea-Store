If you are a passionate baker with a flair for infusing flavors and enjoy creating unique tea-inspired baked goods, we encourage you to apply for the Tea Infused Baker position at THIRSTEA CAT. Join us in crafting extraordinary tea-infused treats that bring joy to our customers.

Key Responsibilities:
Develop and execute recipes for a variety of tea-infused baked goods.
Prepare and bake cookies, cakes, muffins, and other confections.
Infuse baked goods with THIRSTEA CAT teas, ensuring unique and delightful flavors.
Ensure consistent quality, taste, and presentation of all products.
Adhere to safety and sanitation standards in the kitchen.
Collaborate with the team to create new tea-infused recipes.
Keep inventory of baking supplies and ingredients.

Qualifications:
Proven experience as a baker, pastry chef, or relevant role.
Creative flair for developing unique and flavorful recipes.
Knowledge of tea blending and infusion is a plus.
Strong organizational and time management skills.
Attention to detail and precision in baking.
Ability to work in a team and maintain a clean workspace.

Benefits:
Competitive salary.
Opportunity to be part of a socially responsible company.
Employee discounts on THIRSTEA CAT products.
Positive and supportive work environment.
